ORCHIDS THE INTERNATIONAL SCHOOL

Established: 2002

ORCHIDS The International School is one of the best CBSE Schools in Masjid Bunder. ORCHIDS offers education from Nursery to Class 10. It is one of the top emerging CBSE schools to be featured in the Times School Survey 2017. Located in Masjid Bunder, the school excels in blending real-life experiences with theoretical knowledge to develop social competencies in children. It is one of the fastest-growing schools in Mazgaon and nearby areas. At ORCHIDS, we focus on the unique academic philosophy, ‘SHARPER Theory’, that instills in a child the essential values of self-discipline, hard work, and academic rigor. We follow a curriculum based on the CBSE guidelines, specially designed by the Central Administration and Research Team (CART).



SCHOOL LIBRARY

Established: 2004

In my school library, we have various types of books- fictional, non-fictional, literature, books on general knowledge, etc. I love to read storybooks and books on general knowledge which have colourful illustrations. Books in my school library are arranged in such a way that students can find them easily.



SCHOOL AUITORIUM

Established: 2006

Auditorium is situated in the main building in the centre of the school. It has five doors and a number of windows. It is air-conditioned but has electric fans also which are put to work if ever the AC system fails. They are also worked with a generator when there is an electric breakdown and the AC system does not work.

SCHOOL ACHIEVEMENTS

Inter School Speech Competition (Topic: "WHAT IF ?")
Won First Prize In Inter School Speech Competition(Topic: "WHAT IF ?"):

I think that words sums up all that you would feel. My first speech competition was in 4th and I had memorized what I had to say and I remember where I exactly stood it was first. Though what I do remember is how I could not make out if the microphone was receiving my voice properly. I did not know if I was talking clearly. I thought I was talking too fast (as was confirmed by my political science teacher later on). What I do remember is how after I finished and said my winding up argument, my voice continued to stay in a slightly higher pitch (out of nervousness I guess) but at the same time, I wanted to go back and do a better job. I honestly think that's what got me started off.Ever since then be it school or collegiate debates I have always put my name forward. I finished in the top three in most in few I didn't but ever since then each time I have gone up and addressed a different gathering of faces I have always primarily faced that same emotion that hit me that very first time.



Inter School Football Competition
Won Third Prize In Inter School Football Competition:

I have won many other awards in my life but this one is great because I had never won a sport award in my life. When I won this award I had tears of happiness but my other teammates were sad as we finished on 3rd position. I went to my teammates and explained them that winning and losing is a part of a game but the most important is participating. After that they became happy and we celebrated our victory with happiness.
